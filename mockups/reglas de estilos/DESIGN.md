# Design System Strategy: Evergreen & Hearth

## 1. Visual Narrative
The design system utilizes a sophisticated, grounded aesthetic inspired by natural landscapes and heritage materials. By pairing a deep forest green with a warm, creamy neutral, the interface evokes feelings of stability, premium craftsmanship, and organic professionalism. The transition from a spacious layout to a more balanced, standard density ensures that content remains accessible while improving information density for functional efficiency.

## 2. Color Palette
*   **Primary (Forest Green):** `#173124` — A deep, saturated green used for brand identity and high-impact interactive elements.
*   **Secondary (Muted Sage):** `#546257` — A desaturated mid-tone used for supporting UI components and subtle callouts.
*   **Neutral (Warm Cream):** `#f5eedc` — A soft, off-white base that provides a comfortable, low-contrast reading environment compared to pure white.

## 3. Typography
The system uses **Manrope** across all roles to maintain a modern, geometric, and highly legible appearance.
*   **Headlines:** Bold and authoritative, utilizing Manrope's clean structure.
*   **Body:** Optimized for readability with standard line heights.
*   **Labels:** Functional and precise for navigational elements.

## 4. UI Geometry & Spatial Logic
*   **Roundedness (Level 1):** Corners feature subtle rounding. This offers a professional "architectural" feel that is softer than sharp angles but more disciplined than pill-shaped designs.
*   **Spacing (Level 2):** The system employs "Normal" spacing. This provides a balanced information density that feels organized and rhythmic without the editorial airiness of previous iterations, making it better suited for task-oriented workflows.

## 5. Intent and Application
This system is designed for environments where trust and longevity are paramount. It avoids trendy vibrancy in favor of a timeless, high-fidelity color palette and a clean, structured layout.
